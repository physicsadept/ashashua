                                        How Ashashua got it's name..........

 

                                 Long ago when Ashashuaian's were called "beings"  way before the Creampop millenium, and even before the search for Stroodles took effect, a mystical doorway called the "dimentional portal" was sited by one of the local beings. His name was Wings, named by his brother for being full of dreams and liking things with wings. Anyway this young man saw the Portal appear out of no-where. When he saw this he was astonished and told all the other beings in his village that this myserious light force was   radiating,  just south of The Land of Happy Little Trees , when they heard of this,  all the people of the village rushed over to where it had been spotted.

                                   Soon they had realized that these portals were producing strange items. They had definately not come from anywhere on Ashashua. All of the beings that had somewhat studied Ashashua were amazed at this incident. As time passed these people who studied Ashashua became known as the teachers and scholars that devoted every ounce of their time to unlocking the secrets of these portals. These teachers and Scholars began trying to learn the languages of  the text  that was written on most of the items. However their efforts were failing because there were so many different varieties of languages to learn and  so little knowledge about where these items were comming from .

                                     Eventually they had come up with a plan to only learn the one language that seemed to show up the most frequently written on the items. They didn't know what to call the language, but it was obvious that the language was in a classification of it's own. As the years rolled on the pieces of the puzzle about this mysterious language began to be pieced together.

                                     One day,  a scholar (the name of the scholar is unknown and has been lost throughout time) was busy studying this one word he had been working on for a long while, when suddenly he realized how the word was supposed to be said. He took the item that matched the word , and brought it to all the local scholars and teachers and asked if a meeting could take place concerning this new evidence.

                                     At the meeting, the scholar pointed to the item and begun to pronounce it with his mouth. when he spoke he said "isha-shooo!"  After he said this, everyone in the room said "ahhhhhh!" From that day on everone assumed for some reason or another that he said "ish a shooo-ahhhhhh!" The beings on Ashashua decided the day of the meeting that the peoples purpose was to find out more about these portals and to devote most of their efforts to unlocking it's secrets. They decided to name their planet after the first pronounced word only known to who ever, what ever was sending them these strange and mysterious items, from somewhere that was yet to be discovered.

                                  Througout time the original translation of Ashashua was lost. It had gone from "isha-shoo" to "isha-shooo-ahhhhhh" to "Ash-a-shoo-ah" creating the word Ashashua. All because of the scholar on that day of the meeting.

                                  If   the Ashashuaian beings knew that the item the scholar was talking about was an earthling shoe than it could have been named The Planet Of Shoe!!!! Ahhhhh!
